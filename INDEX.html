NIT HAMIRPUR


NIT HAMIRPURalso known as the heaven college for BTech students.
National Institute of Technology (NIT) Hamirpur is a public technical university located in Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, India. It was established in 1986 as a Regional Engineering College (REC) and later became an NIT in 2003. NIT Hamirpur is recognized as an "Institute of National Importance". The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, architecture, humanities, and management.
Key aspects of NIT Hamirpur:
Academic Programs:
NIT Hamirpur offers a wide range of programs including B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, M.Arch, MBA, and PhD degrees. Infrastructure:
The institute boasts a well-maintained, clean, and green campus spread over 320 acres. It features a computer center, a library, an auditorium, an open-air theater, a bank, bookshops, a healthcare center, and sports facilities.
Faculty and Research:
NIT Hamirpur has a strong faculty and focuses on research and consultancy in various fields.
Placements:
The institute has a good placement record with a placement rate ranging from 77% to 89% for B.Tech students, according to a review on Shiksha. The highest package offered to students can be around 18 LPA, with an average package of 8.40 LPA.
Campus Life:
NIT Hamirpur has a vibrant campus life with facilities like hostels, messes, gyms, and sports facilities. Students participate in various events and activities, including NCC, NSS, and annual festivals.
Location:
The campus is located in Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, about 4 km from the main bus terminus, with the Dhauladhar mountain range as its backdrop. NIT Hamirpur is known for its architecture, facilities, faculty, technical excellence, and placements, according to Unstop. It is a popular choice for students pursuing B.Tech degrees.
